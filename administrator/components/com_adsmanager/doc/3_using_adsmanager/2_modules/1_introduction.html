Modules

Introduction

In the free version of AdsManager, 4 modules are installed automatically.

  • mod_adsmanager_ads
  • mod_adsmanager_menu
  • mod_adsmanager_search
  • mod_adsmanager_table

In the other versions, multiple AdsManager modules are installed along with the PaidSystem component.

  • mod_paidsystem_feat
  • mod_advancedsearch
  • mod_adsmanager_adsmap
  • mod_adsmanager_quickicons
